fix(player): resolve hydration playback and volume toggle issues

- PlayerProvider: Implemented Just-In-Time (JIT) audio loading. Bypassed background `engine.load()` during hydration to avoid browser autoplay policy restrictions. Audio files are now dynamically loaded upon initial user interaction (play/seek).
- AudioEngine: Exposed `state()` and `getSource()` methods to accurately track Howler's readiness and support JIT fallback loading.
- PlayerBar: Added `lastVolume` state to properly restore the previous audio level when toggling mute, rather than defaulting to 100%.
- PlaylistDetailPage: Synced UI index by invoking `playList` with the `filteredSongs` array instead of raw `localSongs`, ensuring accurate song playback from sorted playlists.
